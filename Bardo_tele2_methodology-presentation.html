Supplier Confidence Overview

The system automatically prioritizes precision where spend is highest. Apple and Samsung represent 96% of total spend and both achieve 84%+ Grade A/B coverage.

Automatic prioritization: Higher spend suppliers are matched with more specific emission factors and subjected to stricter quality checks. Lower-spend suppliers may use category-average EFs — clearly flagged for potential refinement.

Automated Discoveries

The system doesn't just process data — it actively identifies anomalies, errors, and edge cases that would be missed in manual workflows.

Statement of Account Detection

44 non-invoice documents identified and excluded — prevented false emission entries from account statements.

44 docs44 Statement of Account documents were incorrectly uploaded as invoices. They contain no purchase data and would have generated false emission entries. Detected and excluded automatically.
Quantity Extraction Errors (1000x)

7 activities had "2.100" interpreted as 2.1 instead of 2,100 (European decimal notation). Detected and corrected.

7 fixedActivities where European decimal notation (2.100 = 2,100) was misread as 2.1, resulting in a 1000x quantity error on Power Adapter shipments. All quantities corrected before emission calculation.
Samsung Discount Credit Isolation

242 kickback credits identified as pricing adjustments (not product returns). Unit counts excluded from emission calculations — only the financial impact remains.

242 creditsSamsung kickback credits with unit prices below 20% of market value. These are pricing adjustments, not product returns — units were excluded from emission counts while the financial adjustment remains.
Service Misclassification Correction

58 repair service activities were classified as "Goods" — reclassified to "Services" so the correct emission factor type is applied.

58 correctedAppleCare+, repair services, and ACE programs were incorrectly classified as physical goods. Reclassified to services so the correct emission factor type (spend-based service EF vs product-specific LCA) is applied.
